Week 1: Mon. 12.28.20 - Sun. 01.03.21

LPT: When signing and dating documents, put a zero in front of single digit days. maybe more tinfoil paranoia than a "pro tip", but to avoid having someone forge the date on a document you sign make sure to put a zero in front of the day number if it is a single digit. That way no one can alter an 8 into an 18 or a 28.
